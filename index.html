Who We Are

Body of Christ is a Christian NGO Foundation committed to serving humanity, uplifting the less privileged, and spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ through compassionate action and faith‑based programs.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To demonstrate the love of Christ by serving humanity, empowering communities, and supporting the needy.

Vision: To raise a united body of believers transforming lives through faith, love, and service.
